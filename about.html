I'm Aiyanna — USC Media Arts + Practice '25

I’m a creative designer and media artist with over five years of experience shaping visual identities, websites, and digital experiences. My work brings together storytelling, design, and thoughtful systems to create visuals that feel cohesive, intentional, and human.

I’m especially drawn to building identities, whether for brands, individuals, or creative projects that need a clear visual voice. I enjoy thinking through how a brand looks, moves, and communicates across platforms, from typography and color to layout, imagery, and motion. I care about consistency, but I also care about expression. The balance between clarity and personality is where I feel most at home.

My background spans freelance, agency, and in-house roles, where I’ve worked across branding, web design, e-commerce, and digital campaigns for lifestyle and travel clients. These experiences have shaped the way I approach design: with flexibility, strong communication, and an understanding of how visuals live in the real world.

I approach design as a form of visual storytelling. I think about pacing, hierarchy, and structure the same way I think about narrative. Every element should serve a purpose, guide the viewer, and contribute to a cohesive experience.

Right now, I’m focused on growing a body of work centered on brand identity, web design, and visual direction, while continuing to explore new ways of combining digital craft with creative intuition. I enjoy projects that allow space for exploration, detail, and care.
